Welcome To The Dictionary App! This app helps you find the meanings of words that you are curious about. All you have to do is type your word in the search bar at the top right corner of the screen and press enter. You will see the definition of your word on this page, along with some examples and synonyms. Make Sure To Use The Correct Spelling & Don't Use Any Kind Of Slangs/Abbreviations! Otherwise, you might not get the results you want. Enjoy exploring new words with this app!
